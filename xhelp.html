Frequently Asked Questions

I forgot my password, what should I do?
Use the "Forgot Password" link on the login page to reset it. You'll receive an email with reset instructions.
My recharge hasn't reflected, what should I do?
Wait 15 minutes for processing. If still not reflected, contact support with your transaction reference number.
How long do daily earnings continue?
Each product has a specific earning period mentioned in its description. Check product details before purchasing.
Is there a limit to referrals?
No, you can refer unlimited people and earn from all active referrals in your network.
